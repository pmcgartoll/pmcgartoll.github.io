First.

I'm sure that this site/blog will evolve over time, the inspiration for creating it this weekend was to get myself more comfortable with just putting stuff out there. During covid I've started and stopped so many projects, but I just can't seem to get a project over the line. With this site I hope to just get experience with publishing things. One goal I have is for this site is for it to become something of a personal knowledge graph. I'd like to record thoughts about books I've read, music I've listened to, ideas I find interesting, etc. I'm hoping that publishing these thoughts will force me to be a little more organized and thoughtful. I've tried recording them privately but, since I'm the only one keeping me accountable, it hasn't been very successful. Let's see how this goes!
